# CopperJoint Copy Archetypes

Drop-in templates for the most common writing tasks. Adapt the variables in `{curly_braces}`; keep the structural shape.

## 1. Product page H1 + subhead

**Pattern:**
> # {Action verb} {body benefit} — {hedged efficacy} for {use case}.
> Built with orthopedic guidance to support {comfort/movement/recovery}, whether you're {persona context 1}, {persona context 2}, or {persona context 3}.

**Example (Long Compression Socks):**
> # Long Compression Socks — graduated 15–20 mmHg support for circulation and all-day comfort.
> Built with orthopedic guidance to help support blood flow and reduce leg fatigue, whether you're on a 12-hour shift, on a long flight, or on your feet all day.

## 2. Hero homepage headline

**Pattern:**
> # Compression Designed for {use context}.
> Built with orthopedic guidance, CopperJoint products are made to support {benefit}, whether you're {persona 1}, {persona 2}, or {persona 3}.

(This is essentially the current homepage hero. Use the same shape for landing pages.)

## 3. Benefit triplet (3-up section)

**Pattern (each card):**
> **{icon}**
> ### {2-word benefit phrase}
> {1-sentence explanation that hedges any medical claim}
> [→ {Action link}]

**Example:**
> **stethoscope**
> ### Orthopedic Insight
> Developed with expert guidance to create support that feels thoughtful, comfortable, and wearable.
> [→ Learn More]

## 4. Product card

**Pattern:**
> {Image}
> {Eyebrow: PRODUCT LINE NAME, uppercase, gold}
> #### {Product name}
> {1-line benefit hook, hedged}
> {Price} {strikethrough MSRP if bundled}
> [Add to cart →]

## 5. Email subject lines

**Patterns that work for CopperJoint:**
- `{Use case} got easier — {product}, {benefit}`
- `{Dr. Strasser quote fragment}`
- `Trusted by nurses. Now in {new variant/color}.`
- `Why we replace compression every {N} months`
- `{Persona}'s favorite for {use case}`

**Examples:**
- `Long shifts got easier — our Long Compression Sock is back in pinstripe`
- `Dr. Strasser on why your knee pain peaks at night`
- `Trusted by nurses. Now in graphite and navy.`
- `Why we recommend replacing compression every 4–6 months`
- `A travel sock built for the in-flight crowd`

## 7. Social caption (Instagram, brand voice)

**Pattern:**
> {Hook — single line, no hashtags}
> {Body — 1–3 short sentences}
> {Soft CTA — link in bio or shop name}
> .
> .
> {Tag stack: handle + 3-5 on-brand hashtags}

**Example:**
> 2026 is about moving better — every day.
> At CopperJoint, we're building the next chapter of compression: thoughtful design, clinical input, real-world durability. Trusted by nurses, runners, and the family at home.
> Find your fit — link in bio.
> .
> .
> @officialcopperjoint #compression #recovery #joyinmotion #nursesofinstagram #copperjoint

## 8. Press release lead paragraph

**Pattern:**
> NASHVILLE, TN — {Date} — CopperJoint, the {brand descriptor}, today announced {announcement}. {Single-sentence positioning recap: "Owned by a US family with backgrounds in medicine, caregiving, and athletic performance, CopperJoint continues to deliver on its mission to create products that empower people to move pain-free."}

## 9. Dr. Strasser-attributed content (blog or video)

**Pattern (intro):**
> By Dr. Nicholas Strasser, MD — Board-Certified Orthopedic Surgeon, Fellowship-Trained Foot & Ankle, Chief Medical Advisor at CopperJoint
>
> {Plain-language opening that names the problem the customer is searching for, e.g., "If your heel hurts when you take your first step out of bed in the morning, you're not alone."}

**Pattern (close):**
> Compression is a support tool, not a substitute for medical diagnosis. If your pain is acute, persistent, or accompanied by swelling that doesn't resolve, please consult your orthopedic physician.

## 10. Pitch-deck slide title

**Pattern:**
> Eyebrow (uppercase, gold, 12px): SECTION NAME
> H2 (Ink, Montserrat 600, 28px): {One-line argument}
> Supporting line (Iron text, 16px): {One sentence of evidence}

**Examples:**
- Eyebrow: OPPORTUNITY  /  H2: Tommie Copper's $1.35M FTC settlement left a credibility vacuum. We can own it.
- Eyebrow: WHY US  /  H2: We built this audit in 24 hours. Imagine what we ship in 30 days.
- Eyebrow: 30-DAY DELIVERABLES  /  H2: A rewritten Amazon brand store, an AI Fit Finder demo, and a live marketplace dashboard.

## 11. CTA buttons (on-brand)

**Approved primary CTAs:**
- "Find Relief" (Wellness)
- "Explore Bestsellers" (Everyday)
- "Shop Performance" (Core210)
- "Find Your Fit" (sizing)
- "Build a Bundle" (bundles)

**Approved secondary CTAs:**
- "Learn the Science"
- "Read Full Article"
- "Ask Dr. Strasser"
- "See Customer Stories"

**Avoid:**
- "Buy Now!" (urgency)
- "Click here" (lazy)
- "Get yours today" (TV-infomercial voice)
- "Limited time only" (manipulation)

## 12. Internal / B2B / consulting deliverable header

(Use this when writing decks, reports, or pitch material *for* CopperJoint or *about* CopperJoint.)

> {H1: Deliverable name, chalk on navy, Montserrat 700, 40px}
> {Subhead: 1-line description, chalk-tinted, 16px}
> {Date and version}
