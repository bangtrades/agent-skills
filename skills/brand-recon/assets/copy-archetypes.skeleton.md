# {ENTITY_NAME} Copy Archetypes

Drop-in templates for the most common writing tasks. Adapt the variables in `{curly_braces}`; keep the structural shape.

## 1. Product page H1 + subhead

**Pattern:**
> # {action verb or category label} — {hedged efficacy or value} for {use case}.
> Built with {credibility cue}, {ENTITY_NAME} products are made to {benefit verb}, whether you're {persona 1}, {persona 2}, or {persona 3}.

**Example (lifted from real product copy):**
> # {real example}
> {real example}

## 2. Hero homepage headline

**Pattern:**
> # {Category} Designed for {use context}.
> {benefit + audience}

## 3. Benefit triplet (3-up section)

**Pattern (each card):**
> **{icon}**
> ### {2–3 word benefit phrase}
> {1-sentence explanation that hedges any medical/efficacy claim}
> [→ {Action link}]

## 4. Product card

**Pattern:**
> {Image}
> {Eyebrow: LINE NAME, uppercase, accent color}
> #### {Product name}
> {1-line benefit hook}
> {Price} {strikethrough MSRP if bundled}
> [Add to cart →]
