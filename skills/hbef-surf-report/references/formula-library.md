# HBEF Surf Report Formula Library

Fill-in-the-blank templates for the eight HBEF article archetypes. Variables in `{curly braces}`. For full archetype context, see `article-archetypes.md`. For tone rules, see `voice-and-language.md`. **All formulas cap at 80–150 words.**

---

## F1. Campaign / Program Push

```
{HEADLINE — verb + percentage or program name}

HBEF is {action verb} {specific program/staff line} from {current state} to {target state} for the {school year} school year. {Concrete impact framing — number of students served, campuses covered, why it matters}.

{Optional nested Spotlight Feature — see F2}

{CTA: Donate HERE by [deadline] to help us {hit the goal}!}

Together, we can {mission close — "make Hermosa schools stronger"/"keep every classroom resourced"/etc.}
```

**Filled example:**

> **Help Bring School Counselors to 100%**
>
> HBEF is bridging the gap to move our Hermosa View and Vista counselors from **part-time (80%) to 100% full-time** status for the 2026–2027 school year. Our goal is **3 full-time counselors on-site daily** to support all 1,450 students!
>
> *Counselor Spotlight: Sabrina Okuda — Ms. Okuda has made a tremendous impact on the Hermosa View community through her compassion, leadership, and dedication to students' social-emotional growth.*
>
> **Donate HERE by June 7th to help us get to 100%!**
>
> Together, we can make Hermosa schools stronger.

## F2. Spotlight Feature (nested or standalone)

```
{Spotlight: [Role] Spotlight: [Full Name]}

{Person} has made {a tremendous impact / a meaningful contribution / a lasting difference} {at [school]/in [community]/on [program]} through {their} {compassion, leadership, dedication — pick 2–3 specific qualities}. From {specific example #1 — a program, lesson, or moment} to {specific example #2 — another concrete contribution}, {they / she / he} {helps create / supports / drives} {the outcome — "a positive culture," "students who feel supported," "a thriving program"}.
```

**Filled example:**

> *Counselor Spotlight: Sabrina Okuda*
>
> Ms. Okuda has made a tremendous impact on the Hermosa View community through her compassion, leadership, and dedication to students' social-emotional growth. From leading engaging PBIS SURF lessons on kindness, leadership, and active listening to organizing meaningful schoolwide experiences like our first Career Day, she helps create a positive, connected culture where every student feels supported and valued.

```
{Banner: Surf Report Extra — at top, automatically inserted by HVPTO}

{One-sentence frame: what + when + why.}

{2–3 sentences of specifics: format, dollar at stake, who it serves.}
```

## Headline pattern bank

When stuck on a headline, mine from these patterns:

- **Verb + goal:** "Help Bring School Counselors to 100%" · "Fund a Full-Time Librarian"
- **Equation:** "Summer Plans = Support HBEF" · "Shopping at Lazy Acres = Funding HBEF"
- **Punchy 3-beat:** "Swing Hard. Support Schools. Repeat." · "Walk for Friendship. Walk for Education. Walk to Make a Difference."
- **Question:** "Do You Like Golf? HBEF Has a Spot for You" · "Looking for a Way to Get Involved?"
- **Event + Thanks/Feedback:** "Hearts of Hermosa Thanks" · "HoH Feedback" · "Skechers Walk: The Check Is In"
- **Save-the-Date:** "Mark Your Calendar: [Event]" · "[Event] Is Almost Here"
- **Direct Vote:** "Vote for HBEF at Lazy Acres" · "Tokens to Funds: Vote HBEF"
- **Spotlight:** "Counselor Spotlight: [Name]" · "Meet [Name], HBEF [Role]"

## Length checks before submitting

After drafting, verify:

| Check | Target |
|---|---|
| Total word count | 80–150 |
| Headline word count | 4–8 |
| First sentence length | 20–35 words, ideally with one bold key fact |
| Number of bold phrases | 2–4 (don't over-bold) |
| Number of hyperlinks | 1 primary, max 2 supporting |
| Specific numbers/dates included | At least 1 |
| Named people/sponsors | At least 1 where applicable |
| CTA at end | Yes — link + deadline or action verb |
| Emojis | 0–1 max |
| Hashtags in body | 0 |

If your draft fails any of these, revise before submission.
