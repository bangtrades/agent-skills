# HBEF Facebook Formula Library

Fill-in-the-blank formulas for the most common post types. Use when you need to draft fast. Variables in `{curly braces}`. For full archetype context, see `archetypes.md`. For tone rules, see `voice-rules.md`.

---

## F1. Save-the-Date (event teaser, ~4–8 weeks out)

```
{Event Name}: {Theme Subtitle}
{Day, Date}, {Start–End time} — {Venue}

{1–2 theme-extended sentences about what the night will be. Examples:
- "Get set to sail away with us for an unforgettable night under the stars…"
- "Join us for a night of ocean breezes, incredible company, and…"
- "Mark your calendar… Santa's on his way!"}

{Ticket sale info OR "Details coming soon"}

{CTA LINK}
```

**Filled example:**

> Hearts of Hermosa: Come Sail Away
> Saturday, May 9, 6–11pm — Hermosa Vista School
>
> HBEF is provisioning for our biggest voyage yet: the Hearts of Hermosa gala. It's time to celebrate our community, our schools, and the incredible spirit that makes Hermosa Beach shine.
>
> Tickets on sale: Wednesday, April 1, 10am.
>
> [LEARN MORE]

## F3. Event Recap with reconciled total

```
{Event Name}: The Check Is In: ${Total}! 🎉

A heartfelt THANK YOU to everyone who {theme-extended verbs: "registered, cheered, and supported" / "sailed with us" / "boot-scooted with us"} the {N}{th/st} {Event Name}.

Thanks to your incredible efforts, we raised a record-breaking ${X} for Hermosa schools! {Optional partnership context: "This partnership with {Partner} has now raised over ${X} over {N} years"}, helping HBEF to fund vital educational programs that would otherwise be unavailable to our Hermosa students.

We are deeply grateful to our school administration, teachers, students, families, and everyone who donated or shared {their team links / their support / made it possible}.

Special thanks to our incredible {Event} Co-Chairs, {Name} and {Name}!

Thanks also to: {generous supporters/restaurant partners/key donors list}.

{Theme sign-off: "Well Done, Hermosa!" / "Thank you for sailing with us…" / "Yee-haw, Hermosa!"}

And save the date for the next one: {Date}
```

**Filled example:**

> Skechers Friendship Walk: The Check Is In: $269,812! 🎉
>
> A heartfelt THANK YOU to everyone who registered, cheered, and supported the 16th Annual Skechers Friendship Walk.
>
> Thanks to your incredible efforts, we raised a record-breaking $269,812 for Hermosa schools! This partnership with the Friendship Foundation and Skechers has now raised over $2.13M over 16 years…
>
> Special thanks to our incredible Skechers Walk Co-Chairs, Tricia Scaglione and Joyce Chaput!
>
> Well Done, Hermosa!
>
> And save the date for the next one: Sunday, October 19.

## Theme-replacement shortcuts

For themed events, replace any neutral noun with the themed version. The shortcut table:

| Theme | Generic → Themed |
|---|---|
| **Come Sail Away** (2026 HoH) | bar→galley, ends→anchors down, welcome→all aboard, volunteers→crew, set up→provisioning, hello→ahoy, ticket buyers→sailors |
| **Boots on the Beach** (2025 HoH) | best dressed→saddled up in style, couples→rodeo royalty, outrageous→fashion outlaw, donors→wranglers, dance floor→hoedown |
| **Ocean Air & Epic Beats** (2026 MNO) | drinks→sips, dancing→epic beats, raffle→treasure |
| **Pier-to-Pier Friendship Walk** | walkers→friends, route→friendship trail, finish→checkered flag |

Full theme lexicons live in `theme-extension.md`.

## Length guidance per post type

| Type | Char count target (visible-before-fold) |
|---|---|
| Save-the-Date | 250–400 chars |
| Tickets-on-Sale | 150–300 chars |
| Event Recap | 400–800 chars |
| Check Delivery | 400–700 chars |
| Annual Giving Push | 300–500 chars |
| Sponsor Spotlight | 100–250 chars |
| Volunteer Ask | 150–300 chars |
| Program Update | 80–250 chars (often very short) |
| Last Call | 100–200 chars |
| Photo Gallery Drop | 60–150 chars |

Remember: Facebook truncates at ~480 chars with "See more" link. **Put your headline + hook + CTA in the first 480 chars.** Detail and gratitude lists can go below the fold.
