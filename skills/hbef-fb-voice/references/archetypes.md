# HBEF Facebook Post Archetypes — Full Templates

The 12 recurring HBEF post types with structural templates and real-example excerpts. Use these when you know what kind of post you need. For fill-in-the-blank speed, see `formula-library.md`.

---

## 1. Save-the-Date / Event Teaser

**When:** 4–8 weeks before a major event.

**Goal:** Plant the date, introduce the theme, build anticipation.

**Structure:**
1. Theme name reveal (often in a question or playful intro)
2. Date + time + location (in bold or as a standalone line)
3. 1–2 sentence excitement description that extends the theme
4. Ticket-sale date or "more info coming soon"
5. Image (event poster, usually 1080×1350)

**Template:**
```
[Theme name] is coming! 🪩

[Event] — [Subtheme]
[Day, Date], [Time]
[Venue]

[1–2 sentences of theme-extended excitement: "Get set to sail away with us…" / "Join us for a night of ocean breezes…"]

[Ticket-sale date or "Details coming soon"]

[LINK]
```

**Real example (Hearts of Hermosa 2026, hbef.org):**
> Hearts of Hermosa: Come Sail Away
> Saturday, May 9th, 6-11pm — Hermosa Vista School
>
> HBEF is provisioning for our biggest voyage yet: the Hearts of Hermosa gala. It's time to celebrate our community, our schools, and the incredible spirit that makes Hermosa Beach shine.
>
> Get set to sail away with us for an unforgettable night under the stars, raising much needed funds for our Hermosa schools.

## 2. Tickets On Sale 🚨

**When:** The morning tickets drop — usually a Monday at 10am or similar fixed launch.

**Goal:** Convert the warm audience to a ticket purchase before sellout.

**Structure:**
1. 🚨 alarm with "SET YOUR ALARMS"
2. Exact date and time of sale
3. Ticket-tier breakdown (GA + VIP)
4. First-day incentive (limited edition merch is common — trucker hats, totes)
5. Urgency note ("Sells out every year in record time")
6. CTA button-text on a separate line

**Template:**
```
🚨 SET YOUR ALARMS 🚨

Tickets on Sale: [Day, Date], [TIME]!

[Event Name]
[Date, Time, Venue]

[Tier 1]: $[X]
[Tier 2]: $[X]
[Optional VIP tiers]

[First-day incentive line: "First-day buyers receive [limited edition item]!"]

[Sells-out note: "This event sells out every year in record time. Don't miss your chance to secure your spot!"]

[TICKETS ON SALE: [DATE], [TIME]]
```

**Real example (MNO 2026):**
> 🚨 SET YOUR ALARMS: 🚨 Tickets on Sale: Monday, February 2, 10AM!
>
> This event sells out every year in record time. Don't miss your chance to secure your spot!
>
> Every ticket purchased and raffle entry sold goes directly toward supporting the HBEF.
> Let's raise a glass to our students, our teachers, and a night we won't forget.

## 3. Event Recap / Results 🎉

**When:** 1–3 days after the event closes.

**Goal:** Celebrate the result, name the people, plant next year's seed.

**Structure:**
1. **Headline number** if the total is reconciled — exact dollar figure with exclamation. If still tallying: "Well Done, Hermosa!" group sign-off.
2. "Heartfelt thank you to everyone who…" gratitude beat
3. **Named thank-yous** — Co-Chairs (always by full name), restaurant/vendor partners, individual donors
4. Theme reference reprise — extend the theme one more time for the goodbye
5. **Save the date** for the next iteration

**Template:**
```
[Event Name]: The Check Is In: $[exact total]! 🎉

A heartfelt THANK YOU to everyone who [theme-extended verb — registered, cheered, supported, walked, sailed].

Thanks to your incredible efforts, we raised a record-breaking $[X] for Hermosa schools! [Partnership context if applicable: "This partnership with [partner] has now raised over $[X] over [N] years"], helping HBEF to fund vital educational programs that would otherwise be unavailable to our Hermosa students.

We are deeply grateful to our school administration, teachers, students, families, and everyone who donated or shared their team links. Your support was essential.

Special thanks to our incredible [Event] Co-Chairs, [Name] and [Name]!

Thanks also to the following generous supporters: [List of donors / partners].

[Theme sign-off: "Well Done, Hermosa!" / "Thank you for sailing with us…"]

And save the date for the next one: [Date]
```

**Real example (Skechers Walk 2025 recap):**
> A heartfelt THANK YOU to everyone who registered, cheered, and supported the 16th Annual Skechers Friendship Walk.
>
> Thanks to your incredible efforts, we raised a record-breaking $269,812 for Hermosa schools! This partnership with the Friendship Foundation and Skechers has now raised over $2.13M over 16 years…
>
> Special thanks to our incredible Skechers Walk Co-Chairs, Tricia Scaglione and Joyce Chaput!
>
> Well Done, Hermosa!
>
> And save the date for the next one: Sunday, October 19, 2025

## 4. Check Delivery / Annual Contribution Announcement 📚

**When:** Fall, after Annual Giving + summer events totals reconcile and the official check is presented to HBCSD.

**Goal:** Concretize the impact — translate dollars into specific programs.

**Structure:**
1. "HBEF Contributes $[X] to HBCSD for [year] School Year" headline
2. Thanks to families + sponsors, fundraising goal hit
3. Photo of check presentation (full names of everyone in photo)
4. **Bulleted list** of programs funded for the current year
5. "Without this funding, these programs would not have been possible" sentence
6. Gratitude close

**Template:**
```
HBEF Contributes $[X] to HBCSD for the [Year] School Year

Thanks to the support of our generous Hermosa families and local businesses, we reached our [previous year] fundraising goal! With those donations, we were able to fund over 5% of this year's HBCSD budget.

[Check-presentation photo + caption listing all individuals by full name and title]

HBEF's contribution was able to fund the following programs for the current school year ([year]):

- STEM, Music, Physical Education, & Foreign Language education for elementary
- STEM, Band, Art, Drama, & Foreign Language electives for middle school
- Full-time librarians & counselors for all three campuses

Without this funding, these programs would not have been possible. We are so grateful to our Hermosa community for their generous support!
```

**Real example (Oct 2024):**
> Thanks to the support of our generous Hermosa families and local businesses, we reached our 2023-24 fundraising goal! With those donations, we were able to fund over 5% of this year's HBCSD budget.

## 5. Annual Giving Push

**When:** August opens — September close of the AG window.

**Goal:** Drive donations against the $1.7M goal; emphasize 100% participation.

**Structure:**
1. Progress update — running total + percent of goal
2. $1K-per-child suggested anchor (always framed as suggested, never required)
3. Concrete program list (what AG funds specifically)
4. "Every dollar counts" inclusive close
5. CTA button

**Template:**
```
We've raised $[X] toward our $[Goal] Annual Giving goal — [Y%] of the way there!

[1–2 sentence narrative about progress / momentum]

Every dollar funds programs the state won't — STEAM labs, music, drama, Spanish, librarians, counselors, mental-health support, PE.

Suggested family gift: $1,000 per enrolled child. Any amount is welcome — what matters is that every family participates.

[DONATE NOW]

(Tax-deductible! HBEF EIN 33-0522270.)
```

**Real example (Aug 2024):**
> We raised a record-breaking $508,332—the most we've ever raised in the first month of the school year! This puts us on track to meet our annual goal of $1,500,000.
>
> Your generosity demonstrates that education truly matters in our community.
>
> Every dollar counts! If you haven't donated yet, please consider:

## 6. Sponsor Spotlight

**When:** A Community Partner renews, upgrades a tier, or first joins.

**Goal:** Public thank-you that doubles as visibility for the partner.

**Structure:**
1. "Welcome / Thank you to [Sponsor]"
2. Tier name with $ threshold (canonical: Brighter Benefactor $25K+, Diamond $15K+, Platinum $10K+, Gold $6K+, Silver $3.5K+, Bronze $1.5K+)
3. 1 sentence about what they do / why they're great for the community
4. Link to sponsor

**Template:**
```
🙌 [Welcome / Thank you to] our newest [Tier] level [Community Partner / Brighter Benefactor]: [Sponsor Name]!

[1 sentence about the sponsor's business + community tie.]

Their generous support directly funds [program list / impact].

Learn more: [link]
```

(Use sparingly — these can feel transactional. Better when bundled into a multi-sponsor "thank you to all our Community Partners" post on big-event-day mornings.)

## 7. Volunteer Ask

**When:** Roles open up — Auction Co-Chair, Flash Party host, Crew member.

**Goal:** Convert a curious parent into a volunteer without pressure.

**Structure:**
1. Specific role identification
2. What the role involves (1–2 sentences)
3. **"Inquiring doesn't commit you"** reassurance line — HBEF's signature low-pressure pitch
4. Contact person by name + email
5. Optional theme reference

**Template:**
```
We're looking for [role] for [event]!

[1 sentence about what the role does / why it's fun.]

Contact our [role] Chair, [Name], to discuss. Inquiring doesn't commit you; we're here to help!

[Contact email]
```

**Real example (Flash Parties 2025):**
> Contact our Flash Parties Chair, Erin McMurray-Mendenhall, to discuss your party ideas. Inquiring doesn't commit you; we're here to help!

## 8. Program / Impact Update 📚

**When:** Something tangible lands — a new counselor hired, a STEAM lab opens, a Spanish teacher starts.

**Goal:** Translate AG → concrete impact for kids.

**Structure:**
1. Direct statement of the program/impact
2. Specific schools (View / Vista / Valley) and grade levels
3. Why it matters / what HBEF's role was
4. Gratitude or call-back to community support

**Template:**
```
[Program/Impact statement] 📚

HBEF is working to bridge the gap and ensure that [School(s)] has [specific program / coverage] for the [Year] school year.

[1–2 sentences on the impact: who it serves, what they get, why it matters.]

[Closing: gratitude or call-to-action — "Thank you for making this possible" or "This is why we fundraise."]
```

**Real example (current FB post, scraped on 2026-05-26):**
> HBEF is working to bridge the gap and ensure that View and Vista has 100% counseling support for the 2026–2027 school year. 📚

## 9. Press / External Coverage Share

**When:** Local press (Daily Breeze, Beach Reporter, EasyReader) features HBEF.

**Goal:** Amplify third-party validation without rewriting the article.

**Structure:**
1. Brief setup — what got covered
2. Quote pull (1–2 sentences) from the article
3. Article link + outlet + reporter byline
4. Photo credit if using their image

**Template:**
```
The [Outlet] covered our [event/initiative]! [Reporter Name] writes about [headline of article].

"[Pull quote from article]"

Read the full story: [link]
```

**Real example (Daily Breeze, May 2025):**
> The Beach Reporter | By Michael Hixon — "Hearts of Hermosa celebrated 30 years with a country flare"

## 10. Sign-up Reminder / Last Call

**When:** 1–3 days before a deadline (ticket sale closing, AG window closing, walk registration closing).

**Goal:** Convert procrastinators with soft urgency.

**Structure:**
1. Deadline statement (specific date + time)
2. What they're missing if they don't act
3. Direct CTA with link

**Template:**
```
Last call! [Action] closes [Day, Date] at [Time].

[1–2 sentences about what's at stake / what they get if they act.]

[Theme-extended sign-off if applicable.]

[ACTION LINK]
```

**Soft urgency rule:** "Don't miss your chance" / "Tickets sell out quickly" — never "ACT NOW OR ELSE."

## 11. Behind-the-Scenes / Crew Shoutout

**When:** Mid-campaign — to show the volunteer machine and recruit by example.

**Goal:** Humanize HBEF, recognize the Co-Chairs.

**Structure:**
1. Photo of the team / setup
2. "Meet your [Event] Crew!" hook
3. Names + roles of the key volunteers
4. 1-line gratitude tie back to mission

**Template:**
```
Meet your [Event] Crew! 🚀

[Photo of volunteers]

[Names by role: "Auction Co-Chairs Connie Bern and Lindsay Gott / Décor Chair Genevieve Pujalet / Food & Beverage Chair Sarah Lefton…"]

These [theme-extended noun: "captains" / "crew members" / "wranglers"] make the [event] possible. We're so grateful.

[Optional: ticket link or save-the-date]
```

## 12. Photo Gallery Drop

**When:** A few days post-event when the JFP photos are ready.

**Goal:** Drive traffic to the gallery, reinforce the FOMO.

**Structure:**
1. Short hook ("Hearts is always epic, and that includes the attire—our guests BRING IT!")
2. Photographer credit
3. Direct link to gallery
4. Sometimes paired with a teaser image

**Template:**
```
[Event] [Year] was [theme-extended adjective] 📸

[1 sentence describing the vibe / attire / energy.]

Photos courtesy of Jennifer Faulk Photography.

See the full gallery: [link]
```

**Real example (HoH 2026 page):**

## Composite / hybrid posts

Some real posts blend two archetypes — most commonly:

- **Save-the-Date + Sponsor Spotlight** (when a Brighter Benefactor underwrites an event)
- **Recap + Save-the-Date for next** (the canonical recap closer)
- **Program Update + Annual Giving CTA** (impact → "this is why your gift matters → donate")
- **Volunteer Ask + Behind-the-Scenes** (showing the team while recruiting)

When hybridizing, follow the **dominant archetype's structure**, and slot the secondary beat into the gratitude/specifics section before the CTA. Don't try to do all four beats of two archetypes — the post will become a wall of text.
