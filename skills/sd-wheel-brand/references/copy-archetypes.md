# SD Wheel — Copy Archetypes

Patterns synthesized from live sdwheel.com copy. Substitute bracketed slots.

## 1. Hero

**Pattern:** [2–3 word punchy headline] + [inventory/service/payment triple] + [imperative CTA]
> **Ready To Roll** — Massive Inventory, top-tier service, and easy payment options. Get your wheels & tires now.

## 2. How-it-works (3-step)

1. **Choose Your Perfect [Wheels]** — Thousands of [wheels] to choose from & expert customer service to help you find what fits your vehicle.
2. **Select [Tires] For Your Vehicle** — we can help you find [tires] that fit your budget, lifestyle, and are guaranteed to fit.
3. **Shipped Free To You, Ready To Install** — comes mounted, balanced, and shipped to your door for free.

## 3. Deal/financing banner

> Enjoy as low as 0% APR Financing — Upgrade Now, Pay Later.
**Pattern:** [payment benefit] — [aspiration verb] Now, [relief clause] Later.

## 4. Trust bar

> ★★★★★ 4.9 OUT OF 5 STARS OF 3,386 REVIEWS FROM FACEBOOK & GOOGLE
**Pattern:** stars + rating + review count + named sources. Always name the source platforms.

## 5. Service invitation

> Need help to turn your dream vehicle build into a reality? Our world-class team of passionate vehicle enthusiasts have decades of experience, knowledge, and drive to help. Just pick up the phone and give us a ring now!
**Pattern:** [dream-build question] + [team credibility] + [low-friction channel CTA].

## 6. Social caption (Shorts/TikTok/IG style)

> What a good looking pair! [Vision Wheels] and [Cooper STT Pro Tires] 🤘 Start your build at SDWheel.com
> A beefy setup that's not afraid of a little mud 💪 Start your build at SDWheel.com
**Pattern:** [reaction/compliment] + [wheel] and [tire] combo + emoji + "Start your build at SDWheel.com".

## 7. Product blurb

> The [BFGoodrich T/A KO2]'s are an excellent choice for [grip and dependability]!
**Pattern:** [product] is a(n) [solid/excellent] choice for [two concrete benefits].

## 8. Founder/trust narrative

> Since 2003, SD Wheel has had a mission to build relationships with truck and car enthusiasts… Steve Hamilton started SD Wheel out of his parents' garage and built it into one of the largest wheel and tire operations in the country.
**Pattern:** [since-year mission] + [garage origin] + [scale today]. Use for About sections, pitch decks, PR.

## 9. B2B/wholesale

> Interested in selling top industry brands like [Nitto, Toyo, Venom Power]? Our wholesale program has you covered. Become a dealer and access [dealer resources / real-time inventory / flexible payment].
**Pattern:** [brand-name bait question] + [program promise] + [three operational benefits] + "Become a Dealer" CTA.

## 10. Email subject lines (derived, on-voice)

- "Your [F-150] is ready for this setup"
- "Package math: how you save $[XXX] on [wheels + tires]"
- "In stock and ready to roll: [brand] [model]"
- "0% APR ends [date] — build now, pay later"
