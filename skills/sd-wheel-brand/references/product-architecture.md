## Merchandising rules for deliverables

- Always anchor product presentations to a vehicle context ("for your 2024 F-150"), not bare SKUs.
- Lead with package math (savings from free mount/balance/ship vs. local install).
- Truck-first imagery ordering (trucks → SUVs → cars) reflecting audience mix.
- Surface the fitment guarantee near every buy decision.
