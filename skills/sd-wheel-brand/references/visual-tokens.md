## Palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| SD Orange (primary) | `#FFA500` | CTAs, links, accents, highlights, chart series 1 |

Derived tints for dashboards/charts (stay in family): orange-dark `#CC8400`, orange-light `#FFC04D`, charcoal-light `#4A4A4A`, gray-light `#D9D9D9`.

## Typography

- **Family:** Montserrat for all roles (headings, body, captions, data labels). Google Fonts fallback stack: `Montserrat, 'Helvetica Neue', Arial, sans-serif`.
- **Weights:** 800/700 headings (often uppercase in CTAs), 500/400 body.
- **Web scale (from site):** H1 64px, H2 33px, body 22.4px. For documents/decks scale to: H1 36–44pt, H2 24–28pt, body 11–14pt, preserving the loud H1:body ratio (~3:1).
- Headline style: short, punchy, title case or all-caps ("Ready To Roll", "How It Works", "DO YOU HAVE QUESTIONS?").

## Components

- **Primary button:** `#FFA500` background, `#000000` text, 0px border-radius, bold, generous padding, no shadow. Uppercase acceptable.
- **Secondary button:** `#909090` background, `#FFFFFF` text, 0px radius.
- **Inputs:** white bg, `#555555` text, 4px radius, inset shadow `rgba(0,0,0,0.075) 0 1px 1px inset`.
- **Cards/sections:** square corners, white or charcoal bands; orange used as thin accent bars/underlines, not floods.
- **Spacing:** 8px base unit.
- **Stars/reviews:** gold review-star imagery; 4.9★ trust bar pattern (dark band, white text, stars).

## Layout feel

Bold, high-energy, industrial. Big hero + vehicle selector, horizontal brand-logo carousels, three-step "How It Works" with arrows, testimonial pull-quotes with gold stars, dark footer. Design leans utilitarian retail, not editorial — clarity and deal-visibility over whitespace luxury.
