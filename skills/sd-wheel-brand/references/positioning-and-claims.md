# SD Wheel — Positioning & Claims Discipline

## Positioning statement

For truck and car enthusiasts who want their build done right without fitment risk, SD Wheel is the wheel-and-tire package specialist that ships complete, mounted, balanced, guaranteed-to-fit setups free to your door — backed by 20+ years of enthusiast expertise and 0% financing.

## Competitive frame

Position against **Tire Rack** (research authority, weak on truck culture), **Discount Tire Direct** (price + stores, thin enthusiast cred), **4 Wheel Parts / RealTruck** (breadth, weaker package economics). Never position against Enthusiast Enterprises siblings (Custom Offsets, Fitment Industries, TrailBuilt, Mr. Wheel Deal, Vivid Racing, Only Tires) — they are family, segmented by audience.

## Claims: SAFE to use (substantiated)

- Free mounting, balancing, and shipping on wheel & tire packages (asterisk to FAQ terms)
- Fitment guarantee / "guaranteed to fit" (per site policy; fitment recommendations honored)
- Since 2003 / 20+ years of experience
- 4.9★ across 3,386 reviews **from Facebook & Google** (always name sources and date the count)
- BBB A+ rating (note: not BBB-accredited — don't say "accredited")
- 140+ wheel brands / 100+ suspension brands / "millions of wheel and tire packages" (configuration count)
- 0% APR financing available through Affirm ("as low as," subject to approval)
- Lifetime structural warranty / 1-year finish warranty (per warranty page, partner-brand terms)
- Top 500 Internet Retailer (2019) — date it
- Price match: "find it cheaper and we'll do our best to match or beat it" (keep the "do our best" hedge)

## Claims: DO NOT use (unsubstantiated legacy copy — replace)

| Legacy claim | Replace with |
|---|---|
| "#1 Leading Custom Wheel Brand In The World" | "One of the largest wheel & tire operations in the country" |
| "Lowest priced leader with the fastest shipping in the world" | "Aggressive pricing and quick delivery on in-stock packages" |
| "Millions of people have entrusted us" | "Thousands of five-star reviews" / dated review counts |
| Unqualified "in stock" promises | Stock-status language tied to live inventory ("ships in X–Y days") |

## Sensitive topics (handle with care)

- **Shipping times:** never promise universal speed; the known complaint pattern is backorder slippage. Use ranges and in-stock qualifiers.
- **Returns:** be upfront — 10–25% restocking fee typical (some products higher), outbound shipping non-refundable unless SD's error, refunds 2–3 business days after inspection. SD covers costs when the error is theirs (including fitment-recommendation errors). Don't bury this; surprise fees are the #2 review-killer.
- **Fitment responsibility:** guaranteed when following SD recommendations; customer-selected fitment is customer responsibility. State which mode applies.
- **House brands (ARKON/Anthem/Anovia):** disclose as "our signature brands" — exclusivity is a selling point, not something to hide.

## Regulatory/platform notes

Consumer financing claims must carry Affirm qualification language ("as low as 0% APR," subject to credit approval). Review-count claims should be dated and sourced. California privacy links (CCPA) required on web properties.
