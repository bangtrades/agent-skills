# Sense Neuro — Product Architecture

## The platform (say it this way)
**One RF sensing platform, three care settings.** A reusable control unit pairs with a **single-use disposable headset** containing **nine antennae** that alternately transmit and receive a safe, low-power radio-frequency pulse across the brain. Healthy tissue, hemorrhage, and ischemic tissue alter the RF signal differently; a proprietary algorithm classifies the result.

> Razor / razor-blade economics: durable control unit (capital) + disposable headset (recurring consumable). Anchor investor/commercial narratives here.

## Core specs (marketing-stated, pre-clearance)
- Single scan in **2.5 seconds**; full assessment in **under 4 minutes**
- **360 data points** per scan
- Analyzes the **entire cranial vault**
- Classifies: **ICH · LVO · non-LVO ischemic stroke · TBI / hemorrhage localization**

## The three configurations

| Product (™) | Setting | Job | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| **NeuroHawk™ — Military / Field** | Battlefield, disaster zones, point of injury | Detect & localize hemorrhage / diagnose TBI in field conditions | DoD-funded flagship; lead FDA submission ("Star Wars-like headgear") |
| **NeuroHawk™ — ED / EMS** | Ambulance, emergency department | Rapid stroke-subtype triage (route to correct facility) | In development |
| **NeuSTAT™ — Continuous Hospital Monitoring** | ICU / inpatient | Continuous, non-invasive ICH monitoring between CT/MRI scans | Original lead; basis of first-in-human evidence |

**Naming discipline:** Use ™ on first mention. Preferred parent brand = **NeuroHawk™** for the field/ED scanner family; **NeuSTAT™** for continuous monitoring. Do **not** reintroduce legacy "SENSE device" naming in new external materials except as historical/trial context. When in doubt, lead with "Sense Neuro's NeuroHawk™ platform."

## Evidence in brief (feasibility-stage — frame accordingly)
- **First-in-Human ICH monitoring (2020):** 9 patients · 600 monitoring hours · 3,600 scans · 100% correspondence with CT at 12h (no false pos/neg); detected a 3 mL bleed expansion **5 hours before standard of care.**
- **First-in-Human stroke differentiation:** 15 patients (5 LVO / 5 non-LVO / 5 ICH) at Good Samaritan Hospital + UC; completed.
