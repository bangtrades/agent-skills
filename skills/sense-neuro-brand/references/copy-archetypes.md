## Do / Don't
- **Do** open with the patient/clinical stakes and quantify.
- **Do** pair a commercial quote with a clinical quote in announcements.
- **Don't** claim diagnostic accuracy as fact or imply FDA clearance.
- **Don't** use orange for anything but the primary CTA.
- **Don't** resurrect outdated timelines or legacy "SENSE device" naming.
