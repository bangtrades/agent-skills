# WaiveLabs — Pitch / Sales Deck Spec (.pptx)

Pairs with the **`pptx`** skill (mechanics) and optionally **`client-pitch`** (the AI-transformation
proposal structure). Read those for the file-building; read this for the WaiveLabs look + flow.
16:9, 13.333in × 7.5in.

## Master look

- **Two slide modes:** *Dawn* (light — cream `#FFF8F1` / white, ink `#0F172A` text) for content
  slides; *Dusk* (dark — navy `#0C1428` or dusk slate `#161B26`, `#E9EEF7` text) for the cover,
  section dividers, and the "enterprise" capstone. Alternate to create rhythm.
- **Title bar:** primary horizontal logo lockup top-left (use `waivelabs-logo-light.png` on dark
  slides, `waivelabs-logo.png` on light). Slide number + "WaiveLabs.ai" bottom-right, small, muted.
- **Type:** titles **Sora** 700–800; body **Inter** 400–600; one **Fraunces italic** word allowed on
  the cover / a section divider. Numbers/KPIs in Inter, large and tabular.
- **Accent discipline:** blue is structural; orange is the single spark per slide (one stat, one
  arrow, the `.ai`). Hairline dividers, generous margins, lots of negative space — editorial, not
  busy. Soft shadows on cards only.
- **Signature motifs:** the **Data → Visibility → Agents → Apps → Autonomy** chain as a horizontal
  progress band; the sunrise-over-circuit mark as a faint watermark on dividers; KPI cards with a
  delta pill + tiny sparkline (Inter numerals).

## Recommended narrative arc (sales / proposal deck)

1. **Cover (Dusk).** Mark + `WaiveLabs.ai`, one Fraunces-accented promise line, client name + date.
2. **The wave / why now.** The AI shift in plain terms — one chart or one sentence, not a manifesto.
3. **The problem (their world).** Scattered data, manual ops, AI that forgets — mirror the prospect.
4. **The offer in one line.** The 90-day promise + the dependency chain band.
5. **What we build.** WaiveBoard cockpit + scoped agents + the mobile app — show, don't list (screens).
6. **How it's safe.** Scoped tools · human approval · audit trail (ties to the Control Plane story).
7. **The 90 days.** Phased roadmap (Data → … → Graduation), week bands, milestones.
8. **Proof / the Lab.** "Everything here is clickable" — point at the live demos, not vaporware.
9. **Programs.** Starter / Core / Full as **tiers, NO dollar figures** on shared/public decks
   (pricing only in a private, explicitly-quoted engagement doc).
10. **Graduation.** What the client owns and can do themselves at the end (the differentiator).
11. **Close (Dusk).** One question, the email CTA (`hello@waivelabs.ai`), the mark.

## Rules

- No pricing on any deck that could circulate publicly; programs as tiers.
- Email CTA, never a calendar/booking slide.
- Every claim true; demos/screens labeled "sample data" where fictional.
- Keep one idea per slide; if it needs a paragraph, it needs two slides.
- Export a matching **PDF** (see `documents.md`) for email-safe sharing.
