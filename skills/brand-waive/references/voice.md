# WaiveLabs — Voice, Slogans & Naming

## Personality & tone

**Personality:** Practical · Coastal · Modern · Trustworthy · Forward-looking.
**Voice:** Clear · Helpful · Strategic · Approachable · Confident.

Write like a knowledgeable local who's done the work: plain words over jargon, outcomes over
features, "we'll show you" over "we'll do it for you" (the *graduation*, not dependency, ethos).
Confident, never hypey. The surf metaphor is seasoning, not the whole menu.

## The "no slop" standard (operator-set, non-negotiable)

- Concrete numbers beat adjectives. *"first reply down to 41 seconds"* > *"lightning-fast."*
- A "so what?" for every fact — why does it matter to the owner.
- Em-dash-friendly, short paragraphs. No bullet spam, no corporate filler, no hedging.
- Persuasive/educational pieces **end on a question to the reader**, not a sales pitch.
- Plain English FIRST, the real term SECOND (e.g. "the employee handbook your AI reads — a *skill*").
- Never claim a certification, partnership, usage, or result WaiveLabs doesn't hold.

## Slogans & canonical lines

- **Tagline:** *Ride the AI wave.*
- **Offer (one line):** *In 90 days, we turn your scattered business data into one organized brain,
  build the apps and AI agents that run on it, and teach your team to keep building — so you leave
  an AI power user, not a dependent.*
- **Brand story:** *A new tech wave is coming. WaiveLabs helps SMBs learn to ride it — unlocking
  agentic workflows to work smarter, move faster, and grow stronger.*
- **Dependency chain (a recurring visual + verbal motif):** Data → Visibility → Agents →
  Apps/Autonomy → Graduation.
- **Editorial hooks proven on the site** (reuse the *cadence*, not verbatim everywhere):
  *"A model is a brain in a jar. A harness gives it hands."* ·
  *"Your AI is brilliant for one conversation. Then it forgets you."* ·
  *"Don't retrain the brain. Hand it the playbook."* ·
  *"Most businesses have no idea what can be built. So we built it."*

## The four brand pillars

1. **We help SMBs ride the AI wave.** Agentic workflows. Real results.
2. **Built for practical impact.** Solutions that save time, cut costs, drive growth.
3. **Guiding businesses into the future.** AI made approachable, actionable, built for what's next.
4. **Your trusted co-pilot in AI adoption.** Transparent, reliable, committed to the long term.

## Naming & terminology (spell these exactly)

- **WaiveLabs.ai** — the *i* in Wa·i·ve. Verify every wordmark/headline.
- **The Lab** — the public demo floor (`/demo`). Exhibits = WaiveBoard, Mobile BI, Control Plane,
  Harnesses, Skills, Memory OS.
- **WaiveBoard** — the ops cockpit (internal app + the public demo look-alike).
- **Build your Agent OS** — the configurator / lead magnet (`/build`).
- **Agent OS** — the engagement outcome (the org's data + apps + agents + skills, assembled).
- Capitalize exhibit/product names; lowercase generic concepts (a skill, a harness, an agent).

## Do / Don't

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| "we'll show you," "you'll leave able to…" | "we'll handle it for you" (breaks graduation ethos) |
| outcomes, real numbers, the owner's words | feature lists, jargon-first, hype adjectives |
| one surf metaphor as seasoning | wall-to-wall wave/ocean puns |
| email CTA (`hello@waivelabs.ai`) | "book a slot," Calendly, calendar embeds |
| programs as tiers | dollar figures on public pages |
| honest status ("SOC2 underway") | implied certs/partnerships/results |
